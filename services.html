Paint Protection Film (PPF)

PPF is the ultimate shield for your vehicle's paintwork. Our military-grade urethane film protects against rock chips, road debris, bug acids, bird droppings and minor scratches — with self-healing technology that removes surface swirls with heat.

  • Full body, partial or custom coverage
  • Self-healing topcoat eliminates light scratches
  • Optically clear — invisible protection
  • Stain & acid resistant surface
  • UV protection prevents yellowing
  • 7-year manufacturer warranty

Ceramic Coating

Our 9H hardness ceramic coating bonds at the nano level with your paint, creating a permanent hydrophobic surface. Dirt, water, and contaminants bead off effortlessly — keeping your car cleaner for longer with minimal effort.

  • 9H hardness ceramic formulation
  • Extreme hydrophobicity & self-cleaning
  • Deep gloss enhancement
  • UV, chemical & oxidation protection
  • Reduces wash frequency significantly
  • 3–5 year protection warranty
Starting From ₹12,000

Graphene Coating

The next evolution in paint protection — graphene-infused coatings offer everything ceramic does, plus superior heat dissipation, anti-static properties, and stronger bonding. The pinnacle of automotive protection technology.

  • Graphene carbon matrix technology
  • Anti-static — repels dust naturally
  • Superior heat dissipation
  • Stronger adhesion than ceramic
  • Exceptional water spot resistance
  • 5–7 year protection warranty
Starting From ₹20,000
