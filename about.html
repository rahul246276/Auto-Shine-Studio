About Auto Shine Studio

HomeAbout
Est. 2016

Born From a Passion for Perfection

Auto Shine Studio was founded in 2016 by Rohan Verma — a passionate car enthusiast who believed Delhi deserved a world-class detailing studio that matched international standards. Starting with a small 500 sq ft space in Rohini, we've grown into a 5000 sq ft state-of-the-art facility.

Our philosophy is simple: every car that enters our studio deserves the same meticulous care as if it were our own. We use only internationally certified products and continuously train our team to stay ahead of industry developments.
