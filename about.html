About Auto Shine Studio

HomeAbout
Est. 2016

Born From a Passion for Perfection

Auto Shine Studio was founded in 2016 by Rohan Verma — a passionate car enthusiast who believed Delhi deserved a world-class detailing studio matching international standards. Starting with a 500 sq ft space, we've grown into a 5000 sq ft state-of-the-art facility.

Our philosophy: every car deserves the same meticulous care as if it were our own. We use only internationally certified products and continuously train our team on the latest techniques.
